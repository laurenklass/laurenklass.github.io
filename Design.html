design.

My portfolio showcases various projects created throughout my career through personal work, class assignments, and creations for clubs and events.

VECTOR ART

Vector art, a process done on Illustrator. One of my favorite styles, vector art can take as little or as long as you want it to, depending on how detailed you want your pieces to be. While my piece on Queen Elizabeth was done for a class assignment, the other two were done as personal projects inspired by my travels.

EVENT PROGRAM

School Event: This is a program I created for an annual event hosted at Marshall School of Business at USC. It’s the 2020 Marshall International Case Competition, where students from around the world came to compete in a case competition sponsored by Adobe. I served as Director of External Relations, creating promotional materials as well as a social media challenge.

MENU DESIGN

Triple Threat and a Hidden Joke: Using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, I created a menu for a restaurant I imagined. Color palettes, fonts, and layouts were carefully picked to design a fancy french restaurant. But if you look closely, you’ll realize the descriptions are just fancy ways to describe classic fast foods.

CURRENCY DESIGN

As part of another class project, we were challenged to create currency from scratch. I chose to use one of my favorite artists, Chance the Rapper, as the face of my currency. The currency is valued in “chances” (1 chance, 5 chances, 10 chances) following the name of the artist.
